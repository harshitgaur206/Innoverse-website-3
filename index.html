F.A.Q

QUESTIONS
How can I join Innoverse?+

Innoverse is open to all students of ZHCET, AMU. Visit our Instagram page or fill out the registration form during our annual recruitment drive held at the start of each academic year.

Do I need prior experience in coding?+

Not at all! We welcome students of all skill levels. Our workshops and mentorship programs are designed to help beginners get started and advanced members push their limits.

What kind of events does Innoverse host?+

We organize hackathons, coding contests, tech talks, workshops, project expos, study groups, and inter-university competitions throughout the year.

Can non-ZHCET students participate?+

Our flagship events like Innoverse 2026 are open to students from all colleges. Workshops and internal activities are primarily for ZHCET members.
